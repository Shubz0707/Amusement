BANSHEE PARK

Banshee is a steel roller coaster located at Kings Island amusement park in Mason, Ohio. Designed and built by Bolliger & Mabillard (B&M), Banshee opened to the public on April 18, 2014. The roller coaster features 4,124 feet (1,257 m) of track, making it the longest inverted roller coaster in the world. Banshee is also the first inverted coaster built in the United States since 2006 when Patriot debuted at Worlds of Fun
